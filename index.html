Web development, app development, and ethical hacking each have distinct specializations that contribute to the tech landscape. Web Developer

In summary, a web developer builds and maintains websites and web applications, ensuring they are functional, visually appealing, and user-friendly. App developer

Ethical Hacker

Ethical hackers play a crucial role in cybersecurity by proactively identifying and mitigating potential security risks. By ethically hacking systems with permission, they help organizations strengthen their defenses against cyber threats, ensuring the safety and integrity of digital assets.
